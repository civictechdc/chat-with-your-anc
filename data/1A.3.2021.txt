Commissioner Wray introduced and moved to oppose the plans and zoning relief presented as Exhibit 4 in the Office of Zoning ’s IZIS for BZA-20425 616 Quebec Place NW, LLC to convert an existing three- story, semi-detached, principal dwelling unit to a three-unit apartment house in the RF-1 Zone. The applicant communicated that new plans would be presented at a future meeting 
 Motion in opposition was approved by voice vote, 11 Commissioners voting “Yes ”; 0 voting “No ”; 0 Abstaining. 
Commissioner Wray introduced and moved to oppose the relief requested for BZA-20395 3200 13th Street to convert an existing, semi-detached, three-story commercial building into a three-unit apartment house, with front, side, and rear additions in the RF-1 Zone.due to a lack of community outreach. The applicant communicated that additional outreach would be conducted and the ANC can revisit this application at a future meeting. 
 Motion in opposition was approved by voice vote, 11 Commissioners voting “Yes ”; 0 voting “No ”; 0 Abstaining. 
Commissioner Wray introduced and moved to support BZA-20423 Philip J. Cross 1219 Kenyon St. To construct a two-story rear addition to an existing, attached, two-story principal dwelling unit, and to convert the principal dwelling unit to a three-unit apartment house in the RF-1 Zone. 
 Motion was approved by voice vote, 11 Commissioners voting “Yes ”; 0 voting “No ”; 0 Abstaining. 
Commissioner Wray introduced and move to oppose the relief request for BZA-20433 Prime Realty, LLC 3556 13th St. to construct two, three-story with cellar, attached, two-unit flats in the RF-1 Zone at the current meeting, with the understanding that the ANC will re-address the BZA case in April 2021. 
 Motion in opposition was approved by voice vote, 11 Commissioners voting “Yes ”; 0 voting “No ”; 0 Abstaining. 
Commissioner Wray introduced and move to oppose the relief request for BZA-20420 428 Manor Place LLC to construct a third story addition and a three-story rear addition to an existing, attached, two-story principal dwelling unit, and to convert the principal dwelling unit to a three-unit apartment house in the RF-1 Zone. 
 Motion in opposition was approved by voice vote, 10 Commissioners voting “Yes ”; 0 voting “No ”; 1 Abstaining. 
Adjournment 
Commissioner Rybarczyk announced the next general meeting of ANC 1A is Wednesday, April 14, 2021. This meeting will be held virtually; more information for how to join the meeting will be available at anc1a.org. 
Vice Chairperson Love Wade moved to adjourn the meeting at 11:04 pm.
