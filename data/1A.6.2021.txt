Commissioner Wray introduced and Commissioner Boese moved to oppose BZA Case #20425: 616 Quebec Place NW, LLC To convert an existing three-story, semi-detached, principal dwelling unit to a three-unit apartment house in the RF-1 Zone, and to appeal the original permit.

Motion in opposition was approved by voice vote, 10 Commissioners voting “Yes ”; 0 voting “No ”; 1 Abstaining.

● 
Motion to deny was approved by voice vote, 11 Commissioners voting “Yes ”; 0 voting “No ”; 0 Abstaining. 
Commissioner Brown introduced and moved a Resolution of Support in Response to DDOT ’s Notice of Intent (NOI-21-36-TOA) to Increase No Standing or Parking Anytime Restrictions at the Intersection of 400 Columbia Rd NW and 3000 Warder St NW. 
● 
Motion was approved by voice vote, 11 Commissioners voting “Yes ”; 0 voting “No ”; 0 Abstaining. 
Commissioner Wray introduced and moved a Letter of Support in Response to DDOT ’s Notification for the Installation of the Warder St NW Protected Bike Lane Notice # 21-155-PS. 
● 
Motion was approved by voice vote, 10 Commissioners voting “Yes ”; 0 voting “No ”; 1 Abstaining. 
Commissioner Rybarczyk introduced and moved a letter of support to Council for continuing fare-free Circulator rides. 
● 
Motion was approved by voice vote, 11 Commissioners voting “Yes ”; 0 voting “No ”; 0 Abstaining. 
Commissioner Rybarczyk introduced and moved a Resolution Urging DDOT to Provide ANC 1A with a Comprehensive Proposal of Crosstown Multimodal Transportation Study Implementation and Existing Concepts, and Full Review of its Planned Transportation Networks through ANC 1A. 
● 
Motion was approved by voice vote, 9 Commissioners voting “Yes ”; 0 voting “No ”; 0 Abstaining. 
Commissioner Love Wade introduced and moved to deny a grant application request for Ward 1 Mutual Aid Network, Park View Pod to Mandy Lee for a Mural Painting (Morton Street Kids Camp) for $1,200, with recognition that the request includes a number of items the ANC cannot fund, and will follow up with the applicant and reconsider at a future meeting if appropriate.
