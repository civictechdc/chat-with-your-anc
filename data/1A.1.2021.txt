Official Business 
Commissioner Wray introduced and Commissioner Boese moved to oppose BZA Case 20352 - Application of 426 MANOR PLACE LLC, pursuant to 11 DCMR, Subtitle X, Chapter 9, for special exceptions under the residential conversion requirements of Subtitle U § 320.2, and under Subtitle C § 703.2, from the minimum parking requirements of Subtitle C § 701.5, to convert an existing principal dwelling unit to a three-unit apartment house at in the RF-1 Zone at premises 426 Manor Place, NW (Square 3036, Lot 67) due to issues related to trash and parking. 
 Motion was approved by voice vote, 11 Commissioners voting “Yes ”; 0 voting “No ”; 1 Abstaining. 
Commissioner Wray introduced and moved to support BZA Case 20396 - Raze the existing two-story, attached, principal dwelling unit, and construct a new four-story, nine-unit apartment building with cellar and penthouse, in the MU-4 Zone at 3315 11th Street, NW on the condition that the development team includes a rented parking space in the neighborhood. 
 Motion was approved by voice vote, 12 Commissioners voting “Yes ”; 0 voting “No ”; 0 Abstaining. 
Commissioner Boese introduced and moved a Resolution Requesting DCPS Restore Original Name of Park View School. 
 Motion was approved by voice vote, 12 Commissioners vo ting “Yes ”; 0 voting “No ”; 0 Abstaining. 
Commissioner Wood introduced and moved a Letter of Support to DPR for the 2021 FRESHFARM Farmers Market season at Columbia Heights Plaza. 
 Motion was approved by voice vote, 12 Commissioners voting “Yes ”; 0 voting “No ”; 0 Abstaining. 
Commissioner Miller introduced and moved to support a letter regarding Language Access compliance of information and resources during the COVID-19 pandemic. 
 Motion was approved by voice vote, 12 Commissioners voting “Yes ”; 0 voting “No ”; 0 Abstaining.
